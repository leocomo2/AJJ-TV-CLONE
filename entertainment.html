Entertainment

Influencer Steph Bohrer, 23, has captured the internet’s attention after sharing a touching encounter at the airport.

Known for her travel and lifestyle content, she posted an 11-second video on Instagram and TikTok on March 5, showcasing a note she received while traveling.

In the clip, Bohrer, who boasts one million followers on TikTok and 397,000 on Instagram, explains, “I’m sitting at the airport, and a pilot just placed this on my table and walked away.” The video features a brown paper napkin resting beside a bottle of water.

The handwritten message on the napkin read, “I have seen the whole world, and you are the most beautiful human in it.”

Bohrer, with nearly 780,000 YouTube subscribers, shared a heartwarming video on TikTok featuring Taylor Swift’s “Enchanted” in the background. In the clip, she reveals a note she received from a pilot at the airport, stating, “I didn’t even see his face.”

As of Thursday, March 6, the video had gone viral, garnering 11.4 million views and 1.7 million likes. Bohrer, who resides in Charleston, South Carolina, also uploaded the video to Instagram, captioning it, “So kind.”
